## Opening Cutscene Script

**Scene:** `alaric_workshop` → `millhaven_town_square`

*Fade in. Interior of Alaric's Workshop. Edyn stands at a workbench, examining a broken sundial. Clocks tick on every wall.*

> **Edyn:** (examining sundial) "Hairspring's bent. That's why it won't track. Easy fix."

*Edyn adjusts the sundial. It begins working. A knock at the door.*

> **Bram:** (entering) "Morning, Edyn. Still tinkering away?"

> **Edyn:** "Someone has to fix things around here. What brings you by, Bram?"

> **Bram:** "It's about Alaric. He's been gone three weeks now. The Council is talking about reassigning his workshop."

> **Edyn:** "He'll be back. He always comes back."

> **Bram:** "Edyn... the Chronospire is a long way. And he's not young anymore."

> **Edyn:** "He's fine. The watch is still ticking."

*Edyn holds up the pocket watch from the workbench. It ticks steadily.*

> **Bram:** "I didn't mean to upset you. Just... be prepared, alright? I worry."

> **Edyn:** "I know. Thanks, Bram."

*Bram exits. Edyn looks at the watch.*

> **Edyn:** "Still ticking. That's all I need."

*Pause. Then: THE SHATTERING.*

*Screen shakes violently. All clocks stop simultaneously — visible sprite change. A crack sound effect. The palette shifts — warm tones drain, replaced by cold blue-white. The workshop items visually age/de-age in patches.*

> **Edyn:** "What—?!"

*Edyn runs outside. Scene transitions to `millhaven_town_square`.*

*The town square is in chaos. NPCs are running, some standing frozen in shock. The sky has visible cracks — pale blue Aether energy lines splitting the air. Leaves hang motionless in mid-fall. The town well is flowing upward.*

> **Bram:** (panicked) "The sky cracked, Edyn! The SKY! What in the name of the Architects is happening?!"

> **Old Maren:** (sobbing near her garden) "My herbs! Decades of growth — gone to dust in a heartbeat!"

> **Farmer Hest:** (staring at crops) "Half my field aged fifty years. The other half looks like it was planted yesterday."

*Edyn looks at the pocket watch. It glows faintly and pulls southward.*

> **Edyn:** "The watch is... pulling. Toward the south. Toward Oakwatch Grove."

> **Edyn:** (internal, or spoken softly) "Something's wrong with the Meridians. And if the Meridians are connected to the Chronospire... then Alaric..."

*Edyn goes back into the workshop. Grabs Alaric's Travel Pack from a wall hook.*

> **Edyn:** "I'm going. If the towers are broken, the watch can help. And if the towers lead to the Chronospire... they lead to Alaric."

*Player gains control. Interacting with the south gate triggers:*

> **Edyn:** "Hold on, Alaric. I'm coming."

*Fade out. Quest MQ01 complete.*

## Normal Ending Cutscene Script

**Scene:** `chronospire_stasis` → `millhaven_town_square` (epilogue)

*After defeating Vasael, Edyn descends to the Stasis Chamber. Alaric is suspended in a crystalline stasis field, frozen mid-gesture — he was reaching for something when Vasael froze him.*

> **Edyn:** "Alaric..."

*Edyn holds up the pocket watch. It resonates with the stasis field. The crystal cracks and dissolves. Alaric stumbles forward, disoriented.*

> **Alaric:** "What... where... Edyn?"

> **Edyn:** "I'm here. You're safe now."

> **Alaric:** (looking at Edyn — travel-worn, companions behind them) "You came for me. Of course you did. You never did know when to stop."

> **Edyn:** "The Meridians are restored. All five. The Shattering is... we fixed it."

> **Alaric:** "All five? How long was I—?"

> **Lira:** "Weeks. The whole world fractured. Edyn walked through every broken region to get here."

> **Rook:** "Walked, fought, and annoyed every Keeper along the way."

> **Alaric:** (to Edyn) "I'm proud of you. More than I can say."

*Edyn and Alaric work together to repair the Master Core. The pocket watch serves as a conduit. The Chronospire hums back to life.*

*Montage — each region recovers:*
- Millhaven: Fields growing, Bram laughing, clocks ticking again.
- Stormhaven: Harbor open, ships sailing, sunlight on calm water.
- Ashenveil Marsh: Stillness, peace, mist settling gently.
- Crystalfall Desert: Sand flowing, caravans moving, birds in flight.

*Final scene: Alaric's Workshop. Edyn sits at the workbench, repairing a clock. Alaric watches from a chair nearby, tea in hand. The pocket watch sits on the workbench, ticking steadily.*

> **Alaric:** "You know, I think that sundial you fixed before I left is still running perfectly."

> **Edyn:** (smiling) "Of course it is. I fixed it."

*Camera pans to the window. The Chronospire is visible on the horizon, its lights steady.*

> **Text:** *"Time flows on."*

*Fade to black. Credits roll over companion epilogue montages:*
- Lira treating a patient in a Stormhaven clinic. Pip hands her a bandage.
- Rook hammering at a forge in the mountains, working alone but content.
- Sable leading a caravan through the unfrozen desert, laughing.

*Credits end.*

## True Ending Cutscene Script

**Scene:** `chronospire_heart` → `chronospire_stasis` → epilogue

*After defeating the Heart of the Chronospire:*

> **Heart:** "You stood. Even when I took everything. You stood."

> **Heart:** "The reconfiguration is complete. The Meridians will regenerate Aether from this day forward. The depletion ends."

> **Heart:** "Go to him. He is below. The stasis will release at your touch."

> **Edyn:** "Thank you."

> **Heart:** "Thank you, Edyn. For teaching me what the Architects never could. That the value of time is not in its length, but in what you choose to do with it."

*Path opens. Edyn descends to the Stasis Chamber. Same reunion as normal ending, with one addition:*

> **Alaric:** "The pocket watch — where is it?"

> **Edyn:** "It's... gone. I gave it to the tower. It was the only way to fix the depletion problem."

> **Alaric:** (long pause) "You gave up the watch."

> **Edyn:** "I had to trust that you were alive without it. I couldn't hear your heartbeat anymore. I just... believed."

> **Alaric:** (pulling Edyn into an embrace) "Clocks can be rebuilt. You can't."

*Extended epilogue:*
- All normal ending scenes play.
- Additional scene: Vasael in his Ironpeak cell. Dara approaches. She unlocks the door.

> **Dara:** "The Keepers need help maintaining the new system. You know more about the Meridians than anyone alive."

> **Vasael:** "I don't deserve this chance."

> **Dara:** "No. But the world needs you. Don't waste it."

> **Vasael:** (standing) "...I won't."

- The Chronospire glows warm golden light instead of cold blue.
- Final workshop scene: Edyn is building a NEW pocket watch from scratch. Alaric watches, offering advice.

> **Alaric:** "The mainspring goes there. No — a quarter turn left."

> **Edyn:** "I know. I learned from the best."

*Camera pans to the window. The Chronospire's golden light is visible on the horizon, warm and steady.*

> **Text:** *"Time is not a river flowing toward an end. It is a garden, and someone must tend it."*

*Fade to black. Pause.*

> **Text:** *"Thank you for playing."*

*Credits roll with extended companion epilogues.*

## Shop Dialogue

### Generic Shop Flow

**Greeting:**
> "[Shopkeeper Name]: What can I get for you today?"

**Menu:** Buy / Sell / Leave

**Buy — Insufficient Gold:**
> "You don't have enough gold for that."

**Buy — Success:**
> "Here you go! Anything else?"

**Sell — Item selected:**
> "I'll give you [X]G for that. Deal? Yes / No"

**Sell — Confirmed:**
> "Pleasure doing business!"

**Leave:**
> "Come back anytime!"
